Joining the Tribe

Since I graduated my masters in law and philosophy I realised that I really needed to do something different. I did a career test and the results gave a clear message. GO DO SOMETHING WITH YOUR CREATIVITY! And with technical material. You can do it.

The funny thing was, I had already seen the Growth Tribe Academy 2 months before I graduated. I was very excited about it from the beginning, because it combines a lot of things I like, such as innovation, creativity and a fast changing environment! So I decided to just go for it, and here I am! Excited!!
