Something funny..


Sometimes I mess up Dutch words with English words.. I was in a hotel and my nightlight didn't work.. So I called the reception and asked if they would be able to fix my lamp (pronounced as lamb, dutch word for light). I heard the people on the other side of the line laughing at me and I kept on repeating "lamb, lamb lamb", not knowing what I did wrong, until I realised what I just said in English.
